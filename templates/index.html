ABOUT US

Palatine is research and development team created for a semi-independent territory. Palatine’s contributor can claim their work with pride. The word Palatine carries a weight of history and structure that reflects our mission. Originating from the Latin palatinus ("of the palace"), it historically refers to high-ranking medieval officials or nobles who ruled semi-independent territories known as palatinates.

Orbit-Link

Orbit-Link is a mission design tool that leverages cutting-edge AI to plan space missions. A techno-economic analysis tool built for satellite constellation planning.
